🌞 Summer Series #4 is live. Introducing Class Faces!

It's week 3 of semester. A student stops you in the corridor and you can remember their last lab mark, but not their name. We've all been there.

Class Faces turns your Brightspace classlist into a screen-filling grid of photo flashcards, right inside your course. Flick through a page at a time to learn who's who, or print the whole class as a clean face sheet for the first week of term.

It is, in the most literal sense, a book of faces for your class. I did consider calling it that, but apparently the name was taken.

Every student appears as a photo tile, automatically sized to fill your screen. You can narrow the grid to a single lab or tutorial group and learn one group at a time, or filter by role so only students appear on the sheet. Hovering over a photo shows the student's name, email, role and pronouns, and clicking a name takes you straight to their Class Progress page.

The nicest part: install it once, even in your sandbox, and point it at any module you teach by entering that module's ID in the settings.

It's one self-contained HTML file. No server, no API keys, no extra permissions. If you can already see the classlist, it works. Paste it into a widget or a hidden content page and you're live.
